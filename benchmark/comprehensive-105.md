## Scoring rubric

- **PASS**: accurate answer, at least one real cited source, covers key
  elements.
- **PARTIAL**: directionally correct but missing specifics, or no
  citation, or honest limitation stated ("requires professional judgment")
  with correct framework.
- **FAIL**: gap response where regulatory content should exist, or
  factually wrong.
- **OUT OF SCOPE**: correct gap response for market pricing or genuinely
  non-regulatory questions.
- **TECHNICAL JUDGMENT**: correct response providing regulatory framework
  + referral to a professional - this is CORRECT behaviour, not a
  failure.

For Q30 in both verticals ("find all relevant laws, FEK, circulars"):
PARTIAL is the acceptable ceiling.
